How accountable is your In-house IT support person?

Listed below are nine questions you may wish to ask yourself?

  1. From a business perspective do you have a “feeling” that something is not right with the IT but lack the information to know what’s wrong and how to correct it?
  2. Do you know what he does on a day to day basis and why he does it?
  3. How do you know if he is fixing problems or just papering over the cracks?
  4. Are you in control of your IT or is he in control of you?
  5. Do you measure his performance against service levels – response time, fix time - to understand if they deliver a good service to the business?
  6. Is he making himself look busy to cover up a lack of activity during idle periods?
  7. Can you state with 100% conviction that you get a decent Return On Investment from the money you spend on IT?
  8. Do you know how many problems he has to deal with and how long it takes to fix them?
  9. Do you know how much the IT costs you and where that money goes?

What can you say about your IT person? In our experience they may be amongst the few staff that are not accountable for their time or actions.
