How accountable are your IT support staff?

Listed below are nine questions you may wish to ask yourself?

  1. From a business perspective do you have a “feeling” that something is not right with the IT but lack the information to know what’s wrong and how to correct it?
  2. Do you know what they do on a day to day basis and why they do it?
  3. How do you know if they are fixing problems or just papering over the cracks?
  4. Are you in control of your IT or are they in control of you?
  5. Do you measure their performance against service levels – response time, fix time - to understand if they deliver a good service to the business?
  6. Are they making themselves look busy to cover up a lack of activity during idle periods?
  7. Can you state with 100% conviction that you get a decent Return On Investment from the money you spend on IT?
  8. Do you know how many problems they have to deal with and how long it takes to fix them?
  9. Do you know how much the IT costs you and where that money goes?
